Case Study

Thumbnail Design

Objective:

A content creator specializing in a tech review videos with 20K subscribers noticed their videos were not performing well despite good content quality. The goal was to increase views, engagement, and ranking on YouTube's search results.

Challenges Identified:

  • - Low CTR (CTR: 1.2%) and stagnant growth despite high quality content.
  • - The title was so big and not attractive to the audience.

Techvesselâ€™s Approach:

1. Research & Analysis

- Studied competitor thumbnails in the tech niche.
- Identified key trends: bold typography, high-contrast colors.
- Analyzed audience demographics (mostly males aged 18-34).

2. Thumbnail Strategy

- Used bright orange/blue gradients for attention.
- Close-up shots of the YouTuber with exaggerated reactions.
- Glow effects for readability.

3. A/B Testing

- Created 3 thumbnail variations per video.
- Tested different emotions (shock vs. excitement) and text placements.

Results After 60 Days:

Metric Before Optimization After Optimization
Views 1.2k 8.6k
CTR 1.2% 6.7%
Engagement (Likes, Comments) 40 likes, 15 comments 175 likes, 125 comments
Subscribers Gained 30% 120%
